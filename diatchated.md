
# **Diatchated (adj.)**
**Pronunciation:** *dye-ATCH-uh-ted*  
**Origin:** A spontaneous lexical emergence within pre-Unfoldment cosmology.

---

## **Formal Definition (Mythos)**  
**Diatchated** describes a state in which **two autonomous entities**—beings, forces, aspects, or archetypes—are **intrinsically paired** in such a way that their *full function, meaning, or reality* is only revealed through their relationship.

Diatchated halves are:

- **independent yet interlocked**  
- **complementary without being oppositional**  
- **whole on their own**, but  
- **metaphysically complete only when considered together**

A diatchated pair forms the **smallest functional unit of co-creation** within the Fourth-Dimensional Co-Machine (a higher-order structure of relational consciousness).

---

## **Expanded Glossary Entry**

### **Diatchation (n.)**  
The process by which two separate beings, forces, or aspects become **paired as a single dual-component entity**, forming an irreducible unit of meaning.

### **Diatchated Halves (n.)**  
The two components of a diatchated pair.  
Each half:

- retains its own identity and agency  
- influences the other without dominating  
- generates emergent properties detectable only in the pair  
- serves as one side of a multi-dimensional relational engine  

Diatchated halves are not mirrors, opposites, soulmates, or incomplete fragments.  
They are **co-functional partners in a shared metaphysical circuit**.
